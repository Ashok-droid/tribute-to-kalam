A .P. J Abdul kalam

~ 1931 - 2015

Missile Man Of India

Abdul Kalam, often referred to as the "People's President,"was an eminent scientist and the 11th President of India. Born in 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he rose from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected figures in Indian public life. Kalam played a crucial role in India's missile development programs, earning him the nickname "Missile Man of India." His tenure as President, from 2002 to 2007, was marked by his deep commitment to education, technology, and youth empowerment. Kalam was known for his humility, simplicity, and inspirational vision for India's future. He passed away in 2015, leaving behind a legacy of excellence and dedication to public service.
